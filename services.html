USCIS Support

Expert guidance through immigration processes

Navigating U.S. immigration can be complex. Our experienced team provides comprehensive support for USCIS applications and petitions, including form preparation, document review, and case tracking. We specialize in helping immigrants from East Africa and other regions with their unique immigration needs.

Common Services

  • Form I-485 (Adjustment of Status)
  • Form I-130 (Petition for Alien Relative)
  • Form N-400 (Naturalization)
  • Form I-765 (Employment Authorization)
  • Form I-131 (Travel Document)
  • Form I-90 (Green Card Renewal)

Our Process

  1. Initial consultation to assess your situation
  2. Document checklist and preparation
  3. Form completion and review
  4. Submission and case tracking
  5. Interview preparation
  6. Follow-up and status updates
